ECOKONEK SYSTEM USER MANUAL


Chapter 1: Introduction

1.1 Overview of EcoKonek

EcoKonek is a web-based electronic waste (e-waste) management and community donation platform designed for the Province of Pampanga, Philippines. The system connects citizens who wish to responsibly dispose of their electronic devices with barangay-level administrators who manage collections at designated drop-off centers. Through a gamified approach, users earn Eco-Points and track their environmental impact measured in CO₂ emissions prevented.

EcoKonek also supports the internal asset surrender process of the Clark Development Corporation (CDC), allowing authorized personnel to submit official CDC property for proper disposal with full tracking of property numbers and serial numbers.

1.2 Purpose of This Manual

This User Manual provides step-by-step instructions for all system users—regular users, barangay administrators, and super administrators—on how to navigate and use every feature of the EcoKonek platform. Screenshots referenced throughout this document should be inserted at the indicated placeholder locations.

1.3 System Requirements

To access and use EcoKonek, the following are required:

- A device with a modern web browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, or Safari)
- A stable internet connection
- A valid email address for registration
- JavaScript enabled in the browser

1.4 User Roles

EcoKonek supports three (3) distinct user roles:

Table 1.1 – User Roles and Descriptions

Role                Description
Regular User        A citizen who donates or recycles electronic devices, earns Eco-Points, and tracks environmental impact.
Barangay Admin      A barangay-level administrator who reviews, approves, or rejects donation requests within their assigned barangay. Has access to the Admin Dashboard and CDC Inventory.
Super Admin         A system-wide administrator who can manage donation requests across all barangays, view the Barangay Leaderboard, export CDC reports, and oversee all platform data.

Chapter 3: User Registration

3.1 Creating a New Account

To register for an EcoKonek account:

Step 1: From the Landing Page or Login Page, click the "Sign Up" or "Join EcoKonek" link.

Step 2: The Registration Page will be displayed with the header "Join the Movement."

[Screenshot Placeholder: Registration Page]

Step 3: Fill in the required fields:

Table 3.1 – Registration Form Fields

Field               Requirement
First Name          Required. Enter your given name.
Last Name           Required. Enter your family name.
Username            Required. Must be 3–20 characters long. Only letters, numbers, and underscores are allowed.
Email               Required. Must be a valid email address format.
Phone Number        Required. Must follow the Philippines format (+63).
Password            Required. Minimum of 6 characters.
Confirm Password    Required. Must match the Password field exactly.
Terms & Conditions  Required. You must check the box to agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Step 4: Click the "Create Account" button.

Step 5: Upon successful registration, a green success banner will appear. You will be automatically redirected to the Login Page after three (3) seconds.

[Screenshot Placeholder: Registration Success Message]

3.2 Registering with Social Accounts

EcoKonek supports registration through third-party providers:

- Google – Click the "Continue with Google" button and follow the Google authentication prompts.
- Facebook – Click the "Continue with Facebook" button and follow the Facebook authentication prompts.

Upon successful social registration, you will be redirected to your Dashboard.

3.3 Registration Validation Rules

The system enforces the following validation rules during registration:
- The username must be unique across the platform.
- The email address must not already be associated with an existing account.
- The password and confirmation password must be identical.
- All required fields must be completed before submission.
- The Terms of Service checkbox must be selected.

Step 1: Navigate to the Login Page. The page displays the header "Welcome Back."

9.5.3 Approving a Donation Request

Step 1: Locate the pending donation request card.

Step 2: Click the "Approve" button (green) on the card.

Step 3: The system will:
   - Update the donation status to "Accepted"
   - Recalculate the user's Eco-Points, CO₂ savings, and donation count based on all their accepted donations
   - Refresh the dashboard statistics

[Screenshot Placeholder: Approving a Donation Request]

9.5.4 Rejecting a Donation Request

Step 1: Locate the pending donation request card.

Step 2: Click the "Reject" button (red) on the card.

Step 3: The system will:
   - Update the donation status to "Rejected"
   - Recalculate the user's statistics to exclude the rejected donation
   - Refresh the dashboard statistics

Note: Barangay Admins can only manage donations within their assigned barangay. Super Admins can manage donations across all barangays.

9.6 Exporting CDC Reports

   - Date of submission
   - Device category
   - Brand
   - Model
   - Property Number
   - Serial Number
   - Status

The exported report can be opened in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application.

Chapter 12: Troubleshooting

12.1 Common Issues and Solutions

Table 12.1 – Troubleshooting Guide

Issue                                          Possible Cause                          Solution
Cannot log in                                  Incorrect email or password             Verify your credentials. Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your password.
Social login (Google/Facebook) not working      Browser pop-up blocker enabled          Allow pop-ups for the EcoKonek website in your browser settings.
"Barangay required" warning on Donate page      No barangay selected in profile         Go to your Profile page, click "Edit Profile," and select your barangay.
Donation not appearing in Dashboard             Donation is still pending review        Wait for an admin to approve your donation. Only accepted donations appear in your statistics.
Eco-Points not updating after donation          Donation has not been approved yet       Statistics update only when an admin approves the donation.
Cannot change barangay after selection           Barangay selection is permanent         This is by design. The barangay field is locked after the initial confirmation.
Profile photo not uploading                     File size exceeds limit                 Ensure your image file is under 5 MB.
Password reset email not received               Email went to spam folder               Check your spam or junk folder. Wait a few minutes and try again.
Admin Dashboard shows no data                   No donations submitted yet              Data will appear once users begin submitting donations to your barangay.
Export CDC Report button not downloading         Browser download settings               Check your browser's download settings and allow downloads from the website.
Session expired unexpectedly                    Authentication token expired             Log in again. The system checks token validity on each page visit.

Appendix C: Glossary of Terms

Term                    Definition
Barangay                The smallest administrative division in the Philippines. In EcoKonek, it is used to organize users and donations by locality.
CDC                     Clark Development Corporation. A government-owned and controlled corporation managing the Clark Freeport Zone.
CDC Asset Surrender     The process of officially submitting a CDC-owned electronic device for disposal through the EcoKonek system.
CO₂ Saved               The estimated amount of carbon dioxide emissions prevented by properly donating or recycling an electronic device, measured in kilograms.
Drop-off Center         A designated physical location where users bring their electronic devices for donation or recycling.
Eco-Points              A gamified reward system that assigns points to users based on their donations. Points vary by device type and condition.
E-Waste                 Electronic waste. Discarded electrical or electronic devices and components.
RLS                     Row Level Security. A database security feature used by Supabase to control data access at the row level based on user roles.
Service Role            A privileged database role used internally by the system for administrative operations such as cascading deletions.
Supabase                The open-source backend-as-a-service platform used by EcoKonek for authentication, database management, and real-time data.
